It suggests that residents of Ma On Shan prefer light taste food, as they have Hong Kong-styled canteens most, followed by Japanese, Western and Cantonese-styled ones.People mainly spend on restaurants with prices below 50 HKD, and prices between 51 and 100 HKD.

Therefore, considering the market demand, consumer spending power, and my individual financial capital, a good choice is to open a restaurant offering light meals with prices below 100 HKD.
